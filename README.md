# أمان | Aman

> **"أمان... قرار صحي يبدأ قبل الزواج"**
> *"Aman… a healthy decision that begins before marriage"*

---

## 📖 Project Overview

**Aman (أمان)** is an AI-powered web application designed to support individuals and families in making informed health decisions before marriage.

The platform focuses on simplifying complex genetic medical reports and transforming them into clear, understandable insights that anyone can use — even without a medical background.

In many regions, especially in the Gulf, hereditary blood diseases such as sickle cell anemia and thalassemia are common. Despite the availability of premarital screening, many people struggle to understand their results.

Aman bridges this gap by turning medical data into **knowledge, awareness, and actionable guidance**.

---

## 🎯 What Makes Aman Unique

Aman is not just a report reader — it's a **complete health awareness experience**:

### 🤖 AI-powered analysis
Users can upload their premarital screening reports, and the app automatically reads and explains the results in a simple way.

### 📊 Clear risk insights
Instead of complex terms, users see easy-to-understand indicators like **low**, **medium**, or **high** risk.

### 📚 Educational genetic library
The app provides simplified explanations of common hereditary diseases, including causes, symptoms, and prevention.

### 👨‍👩‍👧 Family-friendly learning
Through stories and interactive activities, even children can understand genetic conditions in an engaging way.

### 🧬 Personal health awareness
Users can track their health information and better understand their genetic status over time.

---

## 💡 The Idea Behind Aman

Many people receive medical test results but don't fully understand what they mean or what actions to take.

**Aman was created to:**

- ✅ Reduce confusion around genetic reports
- ✅ Increase awareness about hereditary diseases
- ✅ Encourage informed and responsible marriage decisions
- ✅ Support healthier families and communities
